What is FTC? Our Mission

The Challenge

The **FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)** is a rigorous robotics competition for students in grades 7-12. Every year, we are tasked with designing, building, and programming a robot to compete in a game with specific objectives and rules. It's about engineering, teamwork, and sportsmanship!

Our Philosophy: "Undo and Redo"

Our team name, **Eagle Works**, represents our commitment to the iterative design process. Failure is just a chance to learn, hit "undo," and come back with a better solution. We prioritize safety, documentation, and sharing our knowledge with the community through outreach events.

Our 2025 Robot: The "Iteration X"

Built for the latest game challenge, Iteration X features a custom 4-wheel drive, a revolutionary scoring mechanism, and advanced autonomous programming using computer vision.

Custom Drivetrain

A highly stable and powerful 4-wheel omni drive system allows for quick, precise movements across the field.

Vision-Based Autonomy

We use OpenCV and TensorFlow to identify game elements, ensuring reliable scoring during the 30-second autonomous period.

Efficient Scoring

Our unique linear slide mechanism and claw assembly allow us to rapidly collect and score game elements at all height levels.
